About

The "Eye Of Nature" project is an independent initiative aimed at strengthening the bond between individuals and nature, which has always been a source of inspiration for humanity. By documenting and exploring the unique nature of each city, John creates a variety of products that help individuals express their creativity. Driven by his love for nature, John's project promotes environmental awareness and encourages individuals to adopt sustainable practices. This commitment is reflected in the way the project operates, with all products being reusable.
